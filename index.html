Majestique Landmarks

Our value-for-money focus stems from a quality policy that consistently surpasses its delivery standards to delight customers at every touch point.

Majestique began its existence as a textile company. Eventually the business grew to include production facilities at Surat, Varanasi, Bengaluru and Mumbai, and 30 retail outlets collectively measuring over 3 lakh sq. ft. in all the major cities of Maharashtra.

In 2007, the company diversified into the real estate sector, with residential projects in Pune, at Hadapsar and Market Yard. The success of the projects prompted a full scale thrust into construction through a variety of residential developments across the city, each aimed at a specific demographic.
